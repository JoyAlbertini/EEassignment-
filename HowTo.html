camelCase vs kebab-case

What's the experiment about?

We will show you a series of words without a particular style and you have to find it hidden in in a bunch of other words in camelCase or kebab-case style. We will measure the time that you require to find such word, in order to undestand which of the two style is more readable. If the task is not clear, don't worry the first two section of the test will surely clear all your doubt, and those are not calculated in the test.
