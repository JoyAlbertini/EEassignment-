camelCase vs kebab-case

What's camelCase?

It's two or more compound words attached toghther with a particular format: the different words are visibly divided by a caps charachter indicating the start of subsequent word. [wikipedia ita] [wikipedia eng]


Example camelCase:

  • 1) words: eat ghost in camelCase : eatGhost

  • 2) words: make step forward in camelCase : makeStepForward

  • 3) words: has eaten more than in camelCase : hasEatenMoreThan


What's kebab-case?

It's two or more compound words attached toghther with a particular format: the different words are visibly divided by a score (-) charachter [wikipedia ita]


Example kebab-case:

  • 1) words: eat ghost in kebab-case: : eat-ghost

  • 2) words: make step forward in kebab-case: : make-step-forward

  • 3) words: has eaten more than in kebab-case: : has-aten-more-than
